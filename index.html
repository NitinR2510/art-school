Art At Creative Spaces

What?

We offer open creative spaces, where kids can just explore and be free.

How?

We offer specific classes as well as special events such as birthday parties and summer camps.

Why?

Because engaging in creative arts promotes self-expression, self-confidence, fine motor skills, social bonding, and so much more!

Where?

The space is located in the heart of Galway and can be easily reached by car and public transport.
