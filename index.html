Art At Creative Spaces

What?

Creative Spaces offers a variety of classes as well as open and creative space for kids to explore their imagination. We also offer space for birthday parties and summer camps.

Why?

Because engaging in creative arts promotes self-expression, self-confidence, fine motor skills, social bonding, and so much more! It's also just a whole lot of fun!!

Where?

The space is located in the heart of Ratzeburg and can be easily reached by car and public transport. Find our address at the bottom of this page.
